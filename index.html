About Me

I am currently a master student at Penn Engineering with a special focus on Computer Graphics. My interests range from Computer Graphics, VR to game design. I am seeking for a technical path in computer graphics and game development.

I discovered my passion for modern video games and its development since high school, and I have learned the foundamentals of game development/game design during my undergraduate at Syracuse, including programming in Unity with C#. During the two years pandemic gap, I have been working in the industry and gained multiple hands-on experience with industry standard 3D game engine and computer graphics software, including both small and large scale game development experience.

Now I am diving deeper into game development and computer graphics at CGGT, and trying the best I can to create some really fascinating projects.

Besides academics, I love to exercise(badminton/running/swimming), read books(mostly fiction), and design games.
